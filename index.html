Don't have your numbers yet? Here's my method

No random calculators. Here's how I find real numbers: ① Track one normal week in a free app (I like MacrosFirst) — every bite, no changes, plus your weight each morning. ② Average the 7 days of calories. ③ Read the trend: weight flat → that average IS your maintenance. Trending up → subtract 100 and re-test a week. ④ For fat loss, eat 10–15% under maintenance, with protein at 0.75–1.2 g per lb (start where you are; add ~15g a week).
